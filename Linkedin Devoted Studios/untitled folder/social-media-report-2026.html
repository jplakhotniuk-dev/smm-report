Monthly analysis & recommendations — DS LinkedIn · March 2026
✅ What's working
People announcements win. Ryan Lastimosa hire: 7,746 impressions & 144 reactions — 5× the channel average. Real names + recognisable franchises trigger organic amplification.
Vulnerability-led case studies drive reach. "We hit a wall" (Secrets of Mimic): 9,971 impressions. Admitting a struggle then solving it outperforms polished success stories.
Team spotlight carousels spike CTR. Ruslana K. post: 56.69% engagement rate, 1,412 clicks. Personal voice + honest advice format resonates deeply with the artist audience.
⚠️ What needs improving
#lighting series over-saturated. 4 posts in one month on near-identical themes (DLSS, fake GI, constraints). Impressions stayed low (980–1,250). Same audience, same message, diminishing returns.
Video underdelivers on impressions. Median impressions for videos: 1,410 — below images (1,430) and documents (2,600). High production cost, lower ROI than carousels.
Double-posting on Mar 20 split reach. Two posts same day: 1,248 + 2,571 impressions. Single strong post would likely outperform both combined.
💡 Ideas & experiments
"Meet the team" series. Replicate the Ruslana K. format for engineers, tech artists, producers. Each post = new person, honest insight, swipeable carousel.
Consolidate #lighting into biweekly deep dives. One strong post every 2 weeks instead of 4 shallow posts per month. Add specific game title context to each.
Let Ryan Lastimosa post himself. First-person post from the new hire's own account, tagged to Devoted — likely 3–5× organic reach vs company page announcement.
