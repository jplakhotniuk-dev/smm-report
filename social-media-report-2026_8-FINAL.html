Layer 2 · Diagnosis What moved · what drove it · is it sustainable?

May paid spend ran — but PLATFORM's biggest result came from $40, not $1,217. Causal read

STUDIO spent $1,217 on boosted posts (Nethunt docs + ArtStation). PLATFORM spent $156 total. The most impactful result of the month came from a post boosted for $40 — which then spread organically to 320K impressions. The boost seeded it; the content carried it.

Driver 1 · PLATFORM viral post
320,198 impressions · $39 boost + organic spread
“Nobody warns you about the 40%” (May 28) — $39.53 boost seeded the initial reach, then organic spread took it to 320K (20× the paid exposure). 3,478 clicks, 90 reactions. PLATFORM's previous best was ~13,000 impr. This is 25× that. Pattern: authentic pain point + specific number + no product pitch.
Driver 2 · ArtStation × PLATFORM launch
+757 PLATFORM followers in 30 days
ArtStation integration launched May 25. “Link your ArtStation to Fusion” post: 13,266 impr, 621 clicks, 4.68% CTR. PLATFORM crossed 2,891 followers — +35% MoM. post0522 "ArtStation × Fusion LIVE" → 10 direct artist sign-ups. The product event did what no amount of cadence posting could.
Driver 3 · STUDIO format consistency
Doc carousels avg 42.7% eng
Even without paid, STUDIO's document carousels continued outperforming: Invincible VS engineering doc hit 50.5%, seasonal skin design 41.2%, character design details 45.5%. The format system is holding. Followers slightly under target (+468 vs +500) purely because no paid campaign ran.
Driver 4 · Ninel follower acceleration
+287 followers · 35,035 impressions · 781 engagements
GamesBeat Summit cluster (3 posts, ~7,257 impr, avg 3.1% eng) + Invincible VS launch (6,020 impr, 175 eng) drove May. Total impressions: 35,035. Key insight: game launches + conference speaking = the two most reliable levers. Personal travel post hit 5,921 impr with zero product content — authentic personal beats polished professional every month.
Verdict: May 2026 is the inflection point for PLATFORM. One viral post + one product launch = 25× impression spike and +35% follower growth. The lesson is not "post more" — it's "post about the problem customers actually feel, then show the product." For STUDIO, the story is quieter: organic-only month delivered 94% of follower target. The 250 free invites that weren't sent (decision #1 from April) cost an estimated 200–300 free followers. That gap is now a documented pattern, not a one-off.
