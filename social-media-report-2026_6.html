Paid ads are carrying 82.6% of all views — and most of that spend is going to a Jul 2024 short ("Entry-Level Game Dev Jobs", 89,642 views in period). Organic channel growth is only 18,407 views across 3.5 months. Ad spend is optimizing for cheap short views, not episode growth.

Full episode cadence is the biggest gap — 1 full episode in 3.5 months vs target of 7. Shorts cadence is also under (avg 2.3/month vs 12 target). Content production pipeline needs to be checked — is this a capacity issue or scheduling?
Shorts with game-recognizable titles outperform. FNAF VR (1,601 views), Overwatch 2 (1,096), Tracer (1,096) all beat generic shorts. Tie future shorts to current game titles DS worked on. The IP association drives Shorts feed discovery.
